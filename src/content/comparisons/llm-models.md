## The Candidates

Choosing the right LLM model for coding is crucial. Here's our detailed analysis of the available options.

## Evaluation Criteria

- **Generated code quality**
- **Context handling**
- **Price per token**
- **Inference speed**
- **Language support**

## Detailed Comparison

### GPT-4 (OpenAI)
The pioneer, still relevant. Excellent for complex tasks but expensive to use.

### Claude 3.5 Sonnet (Anthropic)
Our current favorite. Perfect balance between reasoning and pragmatism. Ideal for clean, maintainable code.

### Gemini Pro (Google)
The surprise outsider. Unbeatable price and advantageous Google Cloud integration.

### DeepSeek Coder
The open source option that rivals proprietary models. Ideal for on-premise deployments.

## Performance Table

| Model | Code Score | Context | Price/M tokens |
|-------|------------|---------|----------------|
| GPT-4 | 92/100 | 128K | $30 |
| Claude 3.5 | 95/100 | 200K | $3 |
| Gemini Pro | 88/100 | 1M+ | $0.5 |
| DeepSeek | 90/100 | 128K | Free |

## Final Verdict

For daily professional use, **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** remains our recommendation. For budget-tight projects, **DeepSeek Coder** or **Gemini Pro** are excellent alternatives.
