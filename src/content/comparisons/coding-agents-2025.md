## The Market Players

The autonomous coding agent segment is experiencing explosive growth. Here's our analysis of the four leading players.

|----------|--------|-------------|-------------------|-------|

## Detailed Analysis

### Cursor
Native VS Code integration makes it the natural choice for most developers. Its familiar interface reduces the learning curve.

### Claude Code
The most powerful in terms of autonomous reasoning. Ideal for complex refactoring and multi-file projects.

### GitHub Copilot Workspace
The advantage of native GitHub integration. Perfect for issue and PR-centered workflows.

### Devin
The most autonomous but also the most expensive. Ideal for deep debugging tasks.

## Our Recommendation

For most developers, **Cursor** offers the best balance of power and accessibility. For complex projects requiring maximum autonomy, **Claude Code** is our choice.
