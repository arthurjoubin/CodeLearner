## Overview

Gemini 3 is Google's flagship multimodal model, setting the standard for native video understanding and massive context processing with up to 2 million tokens.

## Key Strengths

- **Multimodal Excellence**: Native understanding of video, audio, images, and text without conversion
- **Massive Context**: 2M token window for entire books, codebases, or video libraries
- **Real-time Capabilities**: Streaming responses for interactive applications
- **Cost Efficiency**: Competitive pricing for the capabilities offered
- **Google Integration**: Seamless workflow with Workspace, Cloud, and Search

## Architecture

- **Context Window**: 2 million tokens
- **Modality**: Native multimodal (no conversion layers)
- **Training**: Google's latest multimodal corpus
- **Speed**: Optimized for real-time streaming

## Pricing

- Input: ~$0.35 per million tokens
- Output: ~$1.05 per million tokens
- Video/audio: Priced per minute of content
